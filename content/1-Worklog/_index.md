**On this page**, you will need to introduce your worklog. **How** did you complete it? How many weeks did you take to complete the program? **What** did you do in those weeks?

Typically, and as a standard, a worklog is carried out over about 3 months (throughout the internship period) with weekly contents as follows:

**Week 1:** [Getting familiar with AWS and basic AWS services](1.1-week1/)

**Week 2:** [Advanced networking connectivity, VPC, Hybrid DNS & CloudFormation](1.2-week2/)

**Week 3:** [VPC Peering, Transit Gateway, EC2, S3, Storage Gateway & AWS Backup](1.3-week3/)

**Week 4:** [S3 Storage Classes, Snow Family, VM Migration & Hybrid Storage Gateway](1.4-week4/)

**Week 5:** [Amazon FSx, CloudFront CDN & S3 Static Website Hosting](1.5-week5/)

**Week 6:** [AWS Security Fundamentals, IAM, Cognito, KMS, Security Hub & Serverless Automation](1.6-week6/)

**Week 7:** [Advanced IAM, KMS Encryption, CloudTrail, Athena & Cross-Account Access](1.7-week7/)

**Week 8:** [Database Architecture: RDS, Aurora, ElastiCache, Redshift & EC2-to-RDS Applications](1.8-week8/)

**Week 9:** [Database Migration using SCT & DMS: SQL Server, Oracle to Aurora MySQL](1.9-week9/)

**Week 10:** [Data Analytics Pipeline: Kinesis, Glue, Athena & QuickSight](1.10-week10/)

**Week 11:** [NoSQL with DynamoDB, Cloud9, SDK & Multi-Interface Orchestration](1.11-week11/)

**Week 12:** [Advanced ETL, Glue DataBrew, EMR, Kinesis Analytics, Redshift & BI Dashboards](1.12-week12/)
